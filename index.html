About Us

香港科德博市場研究股份有限公司（HK Credible Market Research Co., Limited）是一家立足香港、服務全球的獨立研究與諮詢機構，專注於市場研究、行業分析、企業估值、財務分析及資本市場研究。

科德博（Credible）致力於為上市公司、擬上市企業、投資銀行、私募基金、風險投資機構、會計師事務所、律師事務所及跨國企業提供符合國際標準的獨立研究服務，為企業融資、併購重組、國際化發展及資本市場運作提供專業支持。

我們的研究覆蓋幾乎所有行業，從市場趨勢到消費者行為，通過定製化的研究服務，幫助企業在激烈競爭中做出精準決策。研究遵循獨立、客觀、嚴謹、可驗證的原則，以公開信息、行業數據、企業調研及專業分析為基礎，為客户提供具有參考價值的研究成果。

HK Credible Market Research Co., Limited (香港科德博市場研究股份有限公司) is an independent research and advisory firm headquartered in Hong Kong and serving clients globally, focused on market research, industry analysis, business valuation, financial analysis and capital markets research.

Credible is committed to providing internationally-aligned independent research services to listed companies, pre-IPO enterprises, investment banks, private equity firms, venture capital institutions, accounting firms, law firms and multinational corporations — supporting corporate financing, M&A, international expansion and capital markets operations.

Our research spans nearly every industry, from market trends to consumer behaviour, helping enterprises make precise decisions through customised research services. Our work follows the principles of independence, objectivity, rigour and verifiability, drawing on public information, industry data, company research and professional analysis to deliver findings of genuine reference value.

獨立客觀

Independent & Objective

科德博始終堅持獨立、客觀、公正的原則。除客户依法提供的資料外，研究團隊綜合公開信息、行業數據及自主分析形成研究結論，不受任何第三方的不當影響。一份研究報告的真正價值，不在於它是否"好聽"，而在於它是否"真實"。

Credible always upholds the principles of independence, objectivity and impartiality. Aside from information lawfully provided by clients, our research team forms conclusions based on public information, industry data and independent analysis, free from any undue third-party influence. The true value of a research report lies not in whether it is "pleasant to hear", but in whether it is "true".

Feasibility Study

OUR COMMITMENT TO INDEPENDENCE

Our Commitment to Independence — Credible upholds independence, objectivity and impartiality in every research engagement.

Aside from information lawfully provided by clients, our research team forms conclusions based on public information, industry data and independent analysis, free from any undue third-party influence.

Unless otherwise clearly stated in a report, the Company does not guarantee that any forecast will be realised. Research reports represent professional analytical opinions formed based on information known and reasonable assumptions as of the report date, and do not constitute investment advice, a securities recommendation, or a guarantee of returns.
